--- PLANET PROFILE: GLIESE 581g ---
Gliese 581g is a rocky exoplanet orbiting within the habitable zone of its red dwarf star. The atmospheric composition is heavily saturated with nitrogen and carbon dioxide. Extreme caution: Surface gravity is 1.6x that of Earth. Specialized heavy-exosuits are strictly mandatory for surface exploration to prevent joint collapse.
